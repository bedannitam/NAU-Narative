near future

The pandemic leads to mass layoffs occur. As the economy starts to recover slowly, applicants start reapplying en-masse. The number of applications sent out daily are massive. HR departments are overwhelmed. Demand for fast, efficient processing of applications leads to sped up adoption of AI technologies in recruitment and automated screening.

The EU is setting up new frameworks for automation and worker rights. There are news features that call for more transparency. Demand for laws that enable more accountability..
